Strategic Focus Areas (The "Doc")

Deep dive into specialized funding verticals critical for modern California NPOs in the greater Los Angeles area.

🏅

LA28 Olympics Legacy

The LA28 organizing committee and affiliated foundations are aggressively funding community initiatives that align with Olympic values: youth sports, digital equity, and sustainable community infrastructure.

  • • PlayLA Youth & Sports: Focus on accessibility.
  • • LA84 Foundation: Infrastructure and programs.
  • • Corporate Sponsors: Visa, Delta, Comcast community grants.

Strategy: Position Charges4 Change as a bridge for digital/physical accessibility in local communities ahead of the games.

🏙️

LA Smart City Initiatives

Los Angeles is investing heavily in IoT, connectivity, and data-driven public services. Grants focus on closing the digital divide and creating sustainable urban tech solutions.

  • • California Public Utilities (CPUC): Broadband adoption.
  • • LA Cleantech Incubator (LACI): Pilot project funding.
  • • Civic Tech Microgrants: Community data projects.

Strategy: Align hardware requests (chargers, tech hubs) with the city's broader connectivity and resilience master plans.

🤖

AI & Tech for Good

Major tech players are offering specialized grants (both cash and massive computational credits) to NPOs utilizing AI to solve systemic issues or improve operational efficiency.

  • • Google.org AI Opportunity Fund: Focus on workforce/education.
  • • Microsoft AI for Humanitarian Action: Azure credits & cash.
  • • OpenAI / Anthropic: API credits for non-profits.

Strategy: Apply for software/API grants to automate Charges4 Change administrative tasks or enhance programmatic data analysis.
